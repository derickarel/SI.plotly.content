Marketing Analysis - CVS Store Experience for Gen Z

Marketing Summary: The bar chart indicates that the strengths of the CVS store experience for Gen Z center around excellent customer service, with specific mentions of staff being "great," "helpful," "friendly," and "professional." Personalized interactions, such as remembering names and smiling, are appreciated, as well as speed and convenience. The chart suggests that these elements contribute significantly to a positive perception amongst Gen Z customers. The weakness, as shown by a smaller percentage but nonetheless substantial, is in the drive-thru experience being described as "terrible," signifying an area for improvement.

Marketing Recommendations:

1. Continue to invest in staff training to maintain high levels of customer service, as this is clearly valued by Gen Z customers and differentiates the store experience positively.

2. Recognize and reward employees who create personalized experiences
