1. Prioritize Enhancement of Customer Service:

Many positive reviews refer to great customer service and knowledgeable staff assisting customers in-store. It's important to set a higher standard for customer service training. This will ensure that every customer interaction is positive and encourages repeat business.

2. Improved Store Aesthetics and Cleanliness:

Customers note that clean, well-organized stores have a positive impact on their shopping experience. However, negative comments related to store cleanliness and organization were present. Periodic deep cleaning and consistent tidiness in-store should be prioritized to appeal to shopper's needs for a clean, inviting shopping experience.

3. Enhance Product Availability and Representation:

Some shoppers, specifically within African American demographics, felt the store lacked diversity in their product offerings. Expanding the range of products to cater for a diverse customer base including different cultures, hair types, and skin tones can help everyone feel included.

4. Capitalize on Positive Reviews regarding In-store Services and Experiences:

Comments praised services such as hair styling and brow waxing. In-store service experiences should be promoted and possibly expanded, as these opportunities generate foot traffic and elevate customer satisfaction.

5. Leverage Strength in Gift Cards Sales:

Apparently, customers appreciate the convenience of gift cards for presents. Therefore, efforts to promote gift card sales, especially during holiday seasons or special events, could attract additional business.

6. Improve Training To Prevent Customer Profiling Incidents:

Negative experiences mentioned were around staff profiling customers. Mandatory anti-bias and sensitivity training should be given to employees, fostering an environment of respect, free from discrimination.
