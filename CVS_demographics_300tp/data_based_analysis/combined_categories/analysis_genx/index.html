Gen-X customers at CVS stores have generally praised the company for its excellent and professional customer service, with particular praise given to the friendly and helpful staff, pleasant shopping atmospheres, and well-maintained facilities. However, their criticisms tend to revolve around problematic experiences with the company's shipping services, credit cards, and perception of high prices. Additionally, isolated incidents of rude and unhelpful staff, as well as concerns about store cleanliness and organization, have also been reported. It's crucial for CVS to maintain the top-notch customer service mentioned by many while addressing these valid concerns, thereby enhancing the overall customer experience for Gen-X shoppers.
