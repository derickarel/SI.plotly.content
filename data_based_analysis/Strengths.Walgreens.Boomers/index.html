Marketing Analysis and Recommendations

Marketing Overview

 

The analysis of the attached data looked at four key groups of data: Excellent Location and Pharmacists Arnold, Fantastic Staff, Great Clean Friendly Place, and Awesome Budtender. These labels are the main topics of interest to Boomers when they frequent Walgreens. Based on the evaluated percentage variances, it shows their level of significance to the respective groups.

 

From our observations, the Excellent Location and Pharmacists Arnold was the most significant with a 100%, followed by the Fantastic Staff at 98.01%, Great Clean Friendly Place at 94.82%, and finally, Awesome Budtender at 75.49%. These results indicate a greater importance placed on characteristics like an excellent location and efficient staffing in Walgreens among the Boomer population. The four groups have certain priorities that Walgreens must focus on to maintain and enhance their loyalty and satisfaction.

Marketing Recommendations

 

Recommendation 1:

Focus on improving or maintaining the convenience and accessibility of Walgreens' locations. The "Excellent Location and Pharmacists Arnold" group emerged as the most important factor for Boomers, therefore, ensuring that stores are easily accessible and located close to areas populated by Boomers could be an effective strategy.

 

Recommendation 2:

Increase the visibility and roles of pharmacists such as Arnold who do an excellent job. As Arnold's name emerged in the data, this suggests that personal connection and trust in the pharmacist is very important to the Boomer segment.

 

Recommendation 3:

Continually train and development staff to take "Fantastic Staff" to the next level. The Boomers have shown a high appreciation for well-performing staff which can become a key differentiator for Walgreens in this target population.

 

Recommendation 4:

Maintain exceptional hygiene in all the retail outlets. The "Great Clean Friendly Place" group was quite significant in the data which indicates that Boomer's appreciate a welcoming and clean place to shop.

 

Recommendation 5:

Create a comfortable shopping environment. As shown in the label 'Awesome Budtender,' which might be referring to nice personnel or a soothing in-store ambiance, making the shopping experience pleasant can have a significant impact on customer satisfaction.

 

Recommendation 6:

Perform regular market research and adapt your strategies based on changing preferences. Monitoring voice of customer on a regular basis can help to remain in tune with current trends and changes within the older demographic segment.
