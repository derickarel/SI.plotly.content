Marketing Overview and Recommendations

Marketing Overview

Based on the provided data attached, it seems that one of the major pain points centered around Generation Z customers’ experiences with CVS (specifically through the lens of weaknesses) is the operation of the drive-through service. Many customers voiced dissatisfaction with inefficient operation and long wait times. With labels, "Terrible drive thru" and "Avoid at all cost" being significantly highlighted, it could energetically influence the company's reputation and overall customer retention within this demographic. It's notable to express that the impact of these labels, in relevance to the associated percentage variance, shows an influence on customer behavior in selecting and frequenting their preferred pharmacy.

 

Marketing Recommendations

1: Improve Drive Thru Services: To address the concerns raised by Generation Z customers, first and foremost, CVS should focus on improving the efficiency of their drive-thru service. This entails ensuring that the drive-thru facilities are properly staffed, and that employees are trained to operate in a quick and efficient manner. CVS could consider introducing time management training or creating incentives to encourage faster and more efficient service in this area.

 

2: Implement Advanced Technology: CVS could further improve by implementing advanced technology such as automatic prescription dispensing systems, drive-thru displays showing expected wait times, and touch screen ordering. This not only makes service faster but also accommodates the tech-savvy Generation Z demographic.

 

3: Drive-thru Priority for Prescription Pickups: Ensuring the quick processing of prescription pickups at the drive-thrugh could greatly improve customer satisfaction levels, considering its importance. A significant aspect of CVS operations, providing a dedicated and efficient service for this can greatly increase the reputation among Gen Z consumers.

 

4: Customer Feedback Programs: Implementing a customer feedback program specific to drive-thru services could provide valuable insight into the particular pain points and areas for improvement. Rapidly addressing issues brought up through this system can showcase to the customers the company's commitment to improvement and superior service.

 

5: Social Media Engagement: Increasing engagement on social media platforms popular among Gen Z such as Instagram, TikTok, and Snapchat, and addressing concerns raised on these platforms can help improve the reputation and visibility of the brand within this key demographic.

 

6: Increase Visibility of Changes: When changes are implemented based on customer feedback, make sure they are visible and well-communicated. This rewards those who provided the feedback, improves the customer experience and encourages further customer engagement.
