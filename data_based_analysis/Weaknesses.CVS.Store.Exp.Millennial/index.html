Marketing Overview

The dataset provided offers significant insights into the pivotal points of focus for CVS Store Exp Millennial segment. The elements in 'Label' and their corresponding 'percentage' values refer directly to the weaknesses or key issues specific to this customer segment. By understanding these key areas, marketers can devise effective strategies to address these issues and subsequently improve customer satisfaction and loyalty amongst this demographic.

The data indicates a high degree of dissatisfaction related to poor customer service, notably issues related to answering calls and general communication. There seems to also be extensive unhappiness with the waiting times for service and prescription readiness. Another issue of concern seems to be unavailability and incorrect dispensing of medications. Lastly, negative reviews indicate displeasure towards incorrect store timings and the automated phone system.

Marketing Recommendations

1. Improve Customer Service: CVS should prioritize improving its customer service, especially in the aspects of answering calls and communication. This can be accomplished by hiring more staff, providing adequate training to existing staff, and incorporating a more effective system for answering customer calls promptly.

2. Efficient Prescription Management: The company should take steps to reduce wait times for prescriptions. This can be achieved by implementing efficient inventory management systems, hiring more pharmacists, and improving the overall process related to prescription readiness.

3. Effective Medication Dispensing: To address the problem of incorrect dispensing of medications, CVS should integrate strict quality control checks. Moreover, staff training should emphasize the importance of double-checking medications before they are given to customers.

5. Correct Store Timings: Store hours should be clearly communicated and adhered to. Inconsistencies or inaccuracies in store timings can lead to dissatisfaction amongst customers. This issue can be tackled by ensuring that the operating hours displayed online are accurate and always updated.

6. Revamp Automated Phone System: A significant amount of frustration seems to stem from the automated phone system. CVS could consider making their automated phone system more user-friendly, or offer an option to quickly connect the caller to a live customer service representative for further assistance.
