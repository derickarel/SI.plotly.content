Marketing Overview

 

 

The collected data provides two key implications for GenZ populations relevant to the strengths of Walgreens. The first point of interest or label is "Great place to get", which holds a significant percentage variance of 6.05% for the group. This label is largely associated to healthcare-related contexts such as covid vaccines, flu shots, and booster vaccines. The second point of interest is "Understaffed,", which holds a lower percentage variance of 0.425% for the group. The issues arising from this label are largely related to store management aspects such as stock availability on shelves and staffing.

 

 

Marketing Recommendations

 

 

1. Walgreens can capitalize on its reputation as a "great place to get" vaccines by promoting its healthcare services among Gen Z populations. This could involve campaigns highlighting the ease and professional handling of vaccination services.

 

 

2. In light of the concern around being understaffed, it may be beneficial for Walgreens to invest more in their workforce. This can improve customer experience and address the issue of empty shelves thereby making the shopping experience more satisfactory for the Gen Z population.

 

 

3. Special attention should be paid towards the restocking process. As seen from the reviews, Gen Z customers have mentioned the problem of understocking and empty shelves. Keeping the shelves well-stocked can help in enhancing their shopping experience.

 

 

4. The company might consider providing additional training for employees to ensure high levels of customer service. Feedback noted that the stores were understaffed, which can be addressed by not just hiring more staff but ensuring they are well trained to assist customers.

 

 

5. The issues of understocked and dirty shelves mentioned in the reviews imply an opportunity for Walgreens to enhance its in-store maintenance and cleanliness, providing a more enjoyable shopping experience for its customers.

 

 

6. Given the prominence of the 'great place to get your COVID booster shot' comments, Walgreens might consider fostering this brand image further with targeted advertisements and promotions to their Gen Z audience. Including the ease, professionalism, and speed of their vaccine administration could play a vital role in such campaigns.
