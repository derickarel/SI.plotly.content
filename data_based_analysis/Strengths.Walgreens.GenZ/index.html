Marketing Implication and Recommendations for Walgreens GenZ

Marketing Overview

According to the data provided, there are two distinct areas of significance for the Gen Z demographic relating to Strengths in Walgreens. The first is the perception of Walgreens as a "Great place to get" shots and vaccines, such as those for COVID and flu. This insight is based on the preponderance of reviews in which such 'Product KPIs' like 'shot', 'covid', 'vaccine', 'flu', 'shots', 'booster', and 'vaccines' were mentioned significantly more by the population.

Conversely, the second area of significance is the recurrent mention of Walgreens being "Understaffed," with empty shelves and lacking in product availability. This issue is founded on an array of 'Product KPIs' including 'items', 'stock', 'shelves', 'locked', 'empty', 'everything', 'products', 'buy', 'item', and 'st'. The perception of Walgreens being understaffed and understocked is particularly concerning from a customer service experience standpoint.

Marketing Recommendations

1. With respect to the positive perception of Walgreens' vaccine offerings, marketing efforts should be pivoted towards amplifying Walgreens as a primary destination for all vaccine needs. This strategy could include ads that highlight the convenience, professionalism, and the ease of getting vaccines at Walgreens.

2. It is also recommended that regular updates be sent out regarding the availability of different vaccines (COVID, flu, etc.) at Walgreens' outlets. These updates can be facilitated through mobile apps and emails to keep the Gen Z customers informed.

3. Addressing the understaffing issue raised by Gen Z customers, Walgreen should implement a robust staffing strategy that ensures all outlets are appropriately staffed. This move will not only improve customer service experience but also significantly impact the company's image.

4. To counter the challenge of empty shelves and a lack of product availability, Walgreens should enhance its inventory management system. Such adjustments will ensure that products are always available and meet the demands of the consumers effectively.

5. Incorporating feedback features on Walgreens' platforms will not only encourage Gen Z customers to voice their thoughts, but it will also provide valuable insights for Walgreens to keep improving its service. Soliciting feedback can also elevate the company's image as a customer-centric organization.

6. Lastly, utilizing both digital and traditional marketing channels to communicate improvements made as a result of customer feedback can build a stronger relationship and influence customer retention.
