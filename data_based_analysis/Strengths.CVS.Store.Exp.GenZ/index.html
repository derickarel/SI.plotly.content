Marketing Overview

The data indicates that customer service takes precedence for the majority of CVS Gen Z customers. Many of the customers reviewed positively about the staff's friendliness, helpfulness, and professionalism in assisting them. Moreover, speed, convenience, and the fast pace of service delivery were also identified as vital aspects of the overall customer experience. A few characters were mentioned, such as Cisco and Cheryl, who might have provided exceptional service to customers. Based on top keywords in the reviews, GenZ customers favor attributes such as "great," "helpful," "friendly," "excellent," and "amazing". In terms of general store experience, emphasis was placed on cleanliness, ease of finding products, and the store's atmosphere.

Marketing Recommendations

1. Emphasizing Customer Service: The data suggests that Gen Z customers place a great deal of importance on customer service at CVS. Implementing training programs for employees on how to enhance customer service could significantly improve the customer shopping experience. Employees should be trained to greet customers warmly, answer questions efficiently, and assist customers promptly.

2. Store Atmosphere: It is crucial to maintain a pleasant store atmosphere. This could include playing popular Gen Z music in the store, ensuring the store is clean, and the shelves are well-organized. Regular maintenance of cleanliness should be prioritized.

3. Speedy Service: Gen Z customers appear to appreciate efficiency and speed in service delivery. Work on reducing checkout lines and increasing the number of self-serve checkout kiosks to expedite in-store processes and reduce waiting times for customers.

4. Employee Recognition: Personal mentions of employees like Cisco and Cheryl suggest they provided an exceptional service. Introduce an Employee Recognition Program where customers can nominate employees. Rewarding employees for their service can motivate them and improve overall customer service.

5. Product Availability: Part of the store's appeal lies in its ability to meet customers' needs promptly, as suggested by reviews stating "found exactly what I was looking for". Regular inventory checks and immediate restocking of popular items can further enhance the shopping experience.

6. Online and Mobile Integration: Considering that Gen Z is a tech-savvy generation, integrating the in-store experience with online platforms could be beneficial. Providing a mobile app that can inform customers about the availability of products or self-checkout through the mobile app could improve the shopping experience.
