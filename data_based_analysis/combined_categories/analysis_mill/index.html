Millennials perceive CVS as a reliable one-stop-shop which provides great customer service. They value employees like Margaret and others who go above and beyond in their service. They have a particular appreciation for the clean atmosphere, efficiency, and professional team in the stores. However, Millennials have expressed dissatisfaction with the time-consuming drive-thru services and report that some stores are understaffed.
