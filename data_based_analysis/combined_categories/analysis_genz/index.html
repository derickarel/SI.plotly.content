Based on the data, Gen-Z customers commend CVS for its exceptional customer service, availability of goods, and overall pleasant shopping ambiance, reflected by numerous positive reviews. However, weaknesses such as lengthy phone hold times, unresponsive customer service, issues with drive-thru efficiency, and poor readiness of medications were noted. These areas need improvement to enhance Gen-Z customers' experience in CVS stores. The data indicates that addressing these concerns could significantly uplift the overall perception and usage of CVS stores among this demographic.
