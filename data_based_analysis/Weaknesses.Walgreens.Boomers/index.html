Marketing Overview

 

According to the data provided, two significant areas of concern for the Walgreens boomer population include the concept of 'CUSTOMER' and 'Slowest drive thru'. This conformance to the weakness orientation suggests that this demographic faces challenges related to customer service and the efficiency of the drive-thru service. The explained variance percentages of 0.0033334131209074 and 0.0013645977092983 respectively, although relatively small, indicate a significant impact in the context of customer satisfaction and potential store loyalty.

 

Marketing Recommendations

 

Recommendation 1

In order to improve customer perception and experience, Walgreens could implement a customer service improvement program to better serve their customer base, notably the boomer population. This could involve both an online and in-store approach, to improve the overall customer journey.

 

Recommendation 2

Walgreens should review its drive thru operations for effectiveness. Possible strategies could include ensuring sufficient staffing during peak hours, implementing a more efficient system to handle orders more quickly, or introducing express lanes for simple purchases.

 

Recommendation 3

Providing additional training for staff, particularly in the area of customer service, could be an effective way to address the concerns raised about 'CUSTOMER' experiences. This could enhance staff's abilities to handle customer queries, manage complaints, and maintain a positive attitude even under stress.

 

Recommendation 4

Given the increasingly digital nature of the retail industry, Walgreens could consider improving its mobile and online platforms. User-friendly interfaces and easy navigation would encourage E-commerce amongst the boomer population, which would lessen the need for a physical drive thru presence.

 

Recommendation 5

Walgreens could undertake a customer feedback system, whereby customers can express their experiences and grievances. Regular surveys, a suggestion box or online reviews on the Walgreens app or site would enable the company to receive direct feedback from customers, thus allowing them to improve the targeting of their strategies.

 

Recommendation 6

Walgreens can introduce loyalty programs or discount schemes relevant to the boomer segment. This will not only encourage repeated visits, but also give customers a platform to share experiences and expectations, thus building community.
