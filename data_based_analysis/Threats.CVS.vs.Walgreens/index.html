Marketing Overview and Recommendations

Marketing Overview

The available data presents valuable insights about the public's perception of varied factors within the organizations, Threats, CVS, and Walgreens. The data shows trending topics and areas of interest for the customers, grouped under four categories- 'Best bud tender ever', 'Muy buenos', 'Good for shopping grocery also frozen', 'Terrible photo department' and 'Can't do passport'. Each section has its own level of importance indicated by percentage variance that could help us form a more strategic marketing approach.


The area representing 'Best bud tender ever' stands out most significantly, accounting for the highest percentage variance. This implies that customer service is highly valued and regarded by the customers. On the other hand, areas like 'Terrible photo department', and 'Can't do passport' indicate dissatisfaction in services provided which could be crucial areas to address and improve.


'Muy buenos' and 'Good for shopping grocery also frozen' present a more neutral viewpoint where customers are mentioning these aspects without expressing clear dissatisfaction or extremely positive emotions.

Marketing Recommendations

1. Empower employees and provide necessary training for them to deliver superior customer service that meets or exceeds customer expectations. This could be drawn from the attention 'Best Bud Tender Ever' is receiving, showing that customers value their interactions with employees.


2. Enhance the photo department; investing in better-quality equipment might help improve customer satisfaction. Negative comments regarding this department point out a clear need for improvement.


3. If it's not already being done, both organizations should consider offering passport photo services. This is an additional service that customers evidently want and it's a great way to drive traffic and possibly capture some new customers.


4. In order to keep growing customer satisfaction, consider expanding the grocery section or highlighting the frozen food section more as customers are acknowledging 'Good for shopping groceries also frozen'.


5. Leverage positive feedback and acknowledge the efforts of employees who made a positive impact. Highlight the situations mentioned in positive customer feedback across marketing materials to demonstrate the brand's commitment to customer satisfaction.


6. The data highlights customer demand for a wide range of products. Utilize this in marketing by emphasizing the variety of products available.
