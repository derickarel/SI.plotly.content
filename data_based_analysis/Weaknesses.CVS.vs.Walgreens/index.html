Marketing Overview

 

The data provides an insight into common problems encountered by customers of CVS and Walgreens. It reveals the areas of weakness in communication, customer service, product availability, and acceptance of certain insurance covers. Additionally, there are grievances about early closures ahead of the official closing time and issues with product stock levels. Furthermore, there are complaints about violation of HIPPA policies and issues related to insurance acceptance. Specific praise was noted for exceptional service from certain employees.

 

Marketing Recommendations

 

Recommendation 1: Improve the answering of phone calls. It's clear from the data that customers are frustrated by the lack of response from CVS and Walgreens when they attempt to reach out through telephone calls. Developing a strategy to ensure all calls are answered promptly can improve the brand's reputation and build trust among customers.

 

Recommendation 2: Invest in additional staff during peak hours or develop an efficient staff rotation system to ensure the stores are well staffed at all times. This will mitigate the issues of early store closures and slow service.

 

Recommendation 3: Regularly assess stock levels and ensure popular and essential medications are replenished. A significant number of reviews highlighted the dissatisfaction from customers when common medications are out of stock.

 

Recommendation 4: Conduct regular training for employees on HIPPA policies and other patient privacy rights. It's alarming that the violation occurred in the first place, and it might be indicative of a lack of understanding or adherence to confidentiality regulations.

 

Recommendation 5: Expand on insurance coverage or assist customers better in understanding what their insurance can or cannot cover. Many customers expressed displeasure when their insurances were not accepted.

 

Recommendation 6: Encourage and reward exceptional customer service within the workforce. Specific praise from customers indicates that displays of great customer service do not go unnoticed and can significantly contribute to improving the brand's reputation.
