Marketing Recommendations
  • Address Skin Reaction Concerns: Reviews indicate that customers have experienced negative skin reactions to some CVS competitors products, like allergic reactions and breakouts. CVS should ensure their product selection includes hypoallergenic and non-comedogenic items, and emphasize this in their marketing communication to assure customers of the safety and gentleness of their products.

  • Emphasize Effective Acne Solutions: Some reviews revealed that competitor products were appreciated for their effectiveness in combatting acne. To target this audience, CVS could enhance its range of acne-fighting products, while emphasizing the safety and non-irritating properties of these products in marketing campaigns.

  • Promote Gentle, Non-Irritating Skin Care: Based on the reviews, there is a percentage of customers that are sensitive to harsh, burning or tingly sensations caused by competitor products. CVS could focus a marketing campaign around their gentle skincare line, emphasizing soothing and calming ingredients that do not cause discomfort or irritation.
