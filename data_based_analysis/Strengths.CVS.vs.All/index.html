• CVS should emphasize their range of effective skincare and beauty products. Customers were especially impressed with their facial cleansers and moisturizers, indicating these as 'excellent' and 'gentle yet effective'. For example, comments such as "Excellent gentle cleanser that cleans without drying" and "This gives a beautiful dewy hydrant look to the skin and feels amazing and hydrating" indicate strong customer satisfaction. Prioritizing advertisements for these products could further attract consumers looking for quality skincare.


  • CVS should consider highlighting their variety of lip care products. Reviews such as "Makes your lipsss soft and pink" and "Best lip balm" suggest that customers value these items highly. Marketing campaigns focusing on the effectiveness and benefits of these lip care products would likely resonate with the target audience.


  • CVS might want to emphasize the quality of their overnight skincare products. "Tatcha Indigo Overnight" stood out in reviews, with customers praising its calming and hydrating effects like "Tatcha Indigo Overnight Repair Cream Treatment is wonderful and so calming to my Rosacea skin!!!". Focusing on products that improve the skin's appearance overnight could be a successful marketing strategy.
