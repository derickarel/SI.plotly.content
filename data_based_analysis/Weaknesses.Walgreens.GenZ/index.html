Marketing Overview

The data presented provides a detail assessment of weaknesses experienced with Walgreens by the Generation Z demographic. The following matters were identified as significant points of contention: Waiting in line, poor phone service, ineffective response to calls, poor customer service generally, an overly complicated automated phone system, and lack of responsiveness from the staff.

Each topic is explain by a percentage variance which indicates its significance to the Gen Z group. With these issues identified as top complaints, this provides a targeted strategy for improving service to this demographic. Not addressing these issues could risk threatening existing Gen Z customer relationships and impede the acquisition of new young customers.

Marketing Recommendations

1. Implement Training of Customer Service Representatives: The data reveals several complaints about poor customer service. It is essential to ensure that employees are adequately trained to provide impressive customer service.

2. Improve Phone Service: Complaints surrounding long wait times on call and poor response have been highlighted. It suggests the necessity for a more efficient phone service. A revision of the current phone procedures may be necessary to ensure customer calls are answered promptly.

3. Upgrade Automated Phone System: The automated phone system has been labeled horrible and worst by Gen Z customers. Investing in an updated system that is user-friendly could enhance customer experience.

4. Enhance Queue Management: Waiting in line has been mentioned significantly. Implementing digital queuing systems or self-service kiosks could reduce queuing times thereby enhancing the customer experience.

5. Regularly Monitor Customer Service Performance: The alarming feedbacks from the Gen Z customer base suggests there might be a gap in the monitoring of customer service performance. Regularly monitoring the customer experience can help identify key areas needing improvement and ensure corrective actions are taken promptly.

6. Development of a Customer Complaints Portal: Create a platform where customers can directly raise concerns about their experience. This direct feedback will provide real-time understanding of customer experiences, which can in turn guide swift corrective actions.
