Marketing Strategy for Walgreens GenZ

Marketing Overview

 

Based on the analysis of the attached data, there are noticeable areas of concern regarding the GenZ audience's perception of Walgreens. These areas, labelled as "weaknesses," significantly impact customer satisfaction and loyalty. They are primarily associated with customer service, particularly in areas of long waiting lines, poor response to phone calls, and overall negative reviews of the automated service system. In essence, these problems translate to a potential loss of younger customers, who are integral to Walgreens' current and future market share. This document will outline a comprehensive strategy to address these issues, enhance customer experience, and subsequently increase GenZ market share.

 

Marketing Recommendations

 

1. Improve Customer Service: A significant portion of the criticism is focused on the apparent lack of efficient customer service, particularly in physical stores. Walgreens must invest in intensive customer service training for associates, especially those at the front-line in responding to consumer inquiries and complaints. Timely, efficient, and courteous service can mitigate customer frustration and inspire loyalty.

 

2. Enhance Telephone Services: The data points to growing dissatisfaction with the way Walgreens manage phone calls and requests. This dissatisfaction can alienate GenZ customers who are accustomed to prompt and efficient service due to their high digital engagement. Walgreens need to streamline their phone services, increase staffing where necessary, and ensure calls are answered promptly and professionally.

 

3. Revamp Automated Services: GenZ customers exhibit frustration with the automated voice system, signaling a potential area of improvement. Walgreens should consider investing in cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence services, which efficiently handle customer inquiries, provide accurate information, and reduce operating costs.

 

4. Adopt a Proactive Approach: Tracking negative comments and reviews on various platforms can help Walgreens anticipate customers' needs and concerns, rectify them, and even take advantage of new market opportunities. By displaying proactive engagement, Walgreens can cultivate positive perceptions among GenZ customers and encourage their loyalty.

 

5. Leverage Social Media: Implementing a coherent social media strategy can help Walgreens engage effectively with the GenZ audience. By utilizing popular platforms amongst this demographic, such as Instagram and TikTok, Walgreens can increase brand visibility, engage with customers, address their concerns, and promote services and products.

 

6. Enhance In-Store Experience: Given the complaints about long waiting lines, Walgreens should reassess its in-store operations to ensure that customer wait times are reduced. This reassessment could include increased staffing during busy times, implementing a more efficient queuing system, and enhancing the in-store environment to make any waiting times more pleasant.
