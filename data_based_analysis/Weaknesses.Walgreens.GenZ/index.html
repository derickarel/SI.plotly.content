Walgreens GenZ Marketing Implication

Marketing Overview

The data reveal important marketing insights about the perceived weaknesses of Walgreens among the Generation Z demographic. The areas of weaknesses as identified by the customer reviews include customer service related to phone call response, long waiting times, the inadequacy of the automated phone system, and the general perception of Walgreens stores as places to be avoided. This general negative perception is also reflected in some GenZ customers advocating for others to avoid Walgreens at all costs..

These weaknesses carry significant weight as they inform an overwhelming percentage of negative variance explained for this group. For instance, issues related to "waiting in line" explained 100% of negative variance, while customer service related to phone calls explained between 92 to 59% of negative-variance. Similarly, other identified weaknesses such as the inadequacy of the phone system and the negative perception of Walgreens stores also carried relatively high percentages. Overall, the data shows that these areas of weaknesses are central to the negative perception of Walgreens among GenZ customers.

Marketing Recommendations

1. Improve Customer Service

As identified in the data, customer service is an area of significant weakness. Consider offering customer service training to employees, specifically on handling customer phone calls. Emphasize the importance of quick response times and ensure customers are not left on hold for extended periods.

2. Reduce Waiting Times

Long waiting times, both in lines at the store and on the phone, are a major source of frustration for GenZ customers. Explore strategies for reducing wait times such as introducing more efficient queue management systems within stores and hiring additional staff during peak times.

3. Enhance The Phone System

The data suggests that the automated phone system is a source of frustration for customers. Consider reworking the system to make it more user-friendly, ensuring the system effectively routes calls to human operators more quickly, and eliminating cycles that lead to nowhere.

4. Improve Store Perception

Strive to change the negative sentiment expressed in the phrase "avoid at all costs" through a focus on improving the overall in-store experience. This may require addressing other identified areas of weaknesses such as customer service and waiting times, but also includes the cleanliness, organization, and general atmosphere of the stores.

5. Utilize Social Media

Given the age demographic being focused on, consider utilizing social media platforms to update customers about operation hours and how to best navigate the in-store experience. Prompt responses to complaints on these platforms could also help to mitigate negative perceptions.

6. Feedback Mechanism

Establish more effective mechanisms for receiving and responding to customer feedback. GenZ customers will know their voices are heard, you can identify areas of improvement more quickly, and problems can be resolved before they become widespread.
