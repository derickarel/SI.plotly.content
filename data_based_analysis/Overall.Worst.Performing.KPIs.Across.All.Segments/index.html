Marketing Recommendations

Marketing Overview

     Looking at the various groups of data, it is apparent that the top 4 worst-performing KPIs across all segments largely revolve around poor customer service and slow services. This is reflected in the labels; "Waste of money", "Horrible Customer Service", "Terrible customer service", "Slow". These issues contribute significantly to the overall weaknesses of the organization with implication on the brand's image and customer satisfaction levels. The percentage of variance explained for these groups ranges from 28.4% to 38.5%, indicating that a significant proportion of the performance issues can be attributed to these factors.


Marketing Recommendations

1.      Invest in customer service training and development for employees in all departments. This will help in improving the current 'Horrible Customer Service' and 'Terrible customer service' KPIs.


2.      Implement or adopt a more efficient method of operation to reduce service delivery time, and in turn improve on the KPI labelled 'Slow'. Fast service delivery will not only improve customer satisfaction but also increase the company's competitive advantage.


3.      Use feedback from customers to redesign service procedures or product offerings. Negative reviews such as 'Waste of money' can provide valuable insights into areas of the business or product that need improvement.


4.      Invest in technology that helps automate certain elements of service delivery. This can significantly cut down on time spent waiting by customers, and improve overall effectiveness and efficiency.


5.      Communicate with customers more actively and effectively. Good communication can mitigate the effects of poor customer service and lessen customer frustrations with waiting times.


6.      Consider employing better telephone management systems or expand telecommunication staff. This suggestion stems from the prevalent complaints within most characteristic reviews about non-responsive phone systems or extended hold times.
