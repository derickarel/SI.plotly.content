Tropicana customers highlight their positive experiences by complimenting the high quality of the product, lauding the blend of flavors, and appreciating the brand's positive impact on their health. On the other hand, criticisms focus predominantly on the product pricing and delivery issues. They also mentioned some discomfort from consumption like stomach cramps and in some cases, pets refuse to consume the product. These findings suggest that while Tropicana is revered for its overall quality and taste, attention should be given to packaging and pricing to further enhance the customer experience.
